About Me

I am David, a Games Development student from Mullingar, Ireland. I'm currently studying at Institute of Technology, Sligo with a flair for programming and design. I received 2 awards in LIT's annual Games Fleadh 2018 student competition and can't wait to enter again this year in 2019. I created a custom campaign for the Half-Life mod Cry of Fear in 2012 when I was 15 years old.

My eagerness to learn keeps me captivated in my craft and I love to use my skills to aid remarkable gameplay and enduring narrative-driven experiences. Taking on new challenges is always a joy for me, collaborating together to build something big and exciting is even better!

Here I present to you some of the projects I have had the opportunity of contributing to, as well as some personal fun coding stuff that I've been working on. Every once in a while I like to draw in my free time or for commission work.

If you have any questions, please feel free to get in touch with me!
