STEEL WRATH

Games Fleadh 2018 Project

“Steel Wrath is a sci-fi post-apocalyptic shoot ‘em up game written in C# for MonoGame. The player controls a tank to fight against the hordes of rogue, artificial intelligent tanks on a desolate planet.”

The project was developed on my own during the 2nd year of my Games Development course in IT Sligo. Over a 4 month development period, my role consisted of lead game designer, programmer, artist and video-editing producer. Steel Wrath features smooth tank controls, tank crew power-ups, randomized tank spawns and tank types. I also implemented an A* path-finding algorithm in combination with custom behaviours for the enemy AI tanks. I received 2 awards for this project at Games Fleadh 2018.
